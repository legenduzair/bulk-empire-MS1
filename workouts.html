A Weekly Workout Routine To Get You Started!

  • Three Sets x Until Failure - Underhand Dumbell Flys

Back - Day Two

Arms - Day Five


Dumbell Bicep Curls, Hammer Curls

Legs - Day Six


Barbell Squats, Leg Extensions
