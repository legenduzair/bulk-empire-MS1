A Home Workout Routine To Get You Started!

Chest & Back - Day One


  1. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Bench Press
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Bent Over Rows
  3. Three Sets x 10 Reps - Press Ups
  4. Three Sets x 12 Reps - Dumbell Pullovers

Shoulders - Day Two


  1. Four Sets x 8 Reps - Standing Shoulder Press
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Arnold Press
  3. Four Sets x 12 Reps - Lateral Dumbell Raises
  4. Three Sets x 10 Reps - Front Raises

Arms - Day Three


  1. Three Sets x 10 Reps - Alternating Bicep Curls
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Overhead Tricep Extensions
  3. Three Sets x 10 Reps - Hammer Curls
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Bench Dips

Legs & Abs - Day Four


  1. Three Sets x 10 Reps - Dumbell Squats
  2. Three Sets x 8 - Dumbell Lunges
  3. Three x 10 Reps - Romanian Deadlifts
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Abdominal Crunches

Workouts can be done with dumbells, resistance bands or anything else that makes you feel comfortable!
