A Weekly Workout Routine To Get You Started!

Chest - Day One


  1. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Flat Dumbell Bench Press
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Incline Barbell Bench Press
  3. Four Sets x 10 Reps - Cable Chest Flys
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Underhand Dumbell Flys

Shoulders - Day Three


  1. Four Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Shoulder Press
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Arnold Press
  3. Four Sets x 12 Reps - Lateral Dumbell Raises
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Front Raises

Back - Day Two


  1. Four Sets x 10 Reps - Lateral Pulldowns
  2. Three Sets x 8 Reps - Barbell Deadlifts
  3. Four Sets x 12 Reps - One Arm Dumbell Row
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Pull Ups

Arms - Day Five


  1. Four Sets x 10 Reps - Barbell Curls
  2. Four Sets x 8 - Rope Hammer Curls
  3. Four Sets x 12 Reps - Rope Tricep Extensions
  4. Three Sets x Until Failure - Push Ups

Legs & Abs - Day Six


  1. Four Sets x 12 Reps - Seated Leg Press
  2. Four Sets x 12 Reps - Barbell Squats
  3. Four Sets x 15 Reps - Leg Extensions
  4. Five Sets x 15 Reps - Abdominal Crunches
