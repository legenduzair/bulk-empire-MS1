A Weekly Workout Routine To Get You Started!

Chest - Day One


  • Three Sets x 8 Reps - Flat Dumbell Bench Press
  • Three Sets x 8 Reps - Incline Barbell Bench Press
  • Four Sets x 10 Reps - Cable Chest Flys
  • Three Sets x Until Failure - Underhand Dumbell Flys

Shoulders - Day Three


  • Four Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Shoulder Press
  • Three Sets x 8 Reps - Dumbell Arnold Press
  • Four Sets x 12 Reps - Lateral Dumbell Raises
  • Three Sets x Until Failure - Front Raises

Back - Day Two


  • Four Sets x 10 Reps - Lateral Pulldowns
  • Three Sets x 8 Reps - Barbell Deadlifts
  • Four Sets x 12 Reps - One Arm Dumbell Row
  • Three Sets x Until Failure - Pull Ups

Arms - Day Five


  • Four Sets x 10 Reps - Barbell Curls
  • Four Sets x 8 - Rope Hammer Curls
  • Four Sets x 12 Reps - Rope Tricep Extensions
  • Three Sets x Until Failure - Push Ups

Legs & Abs - Day Six


  • Four Sets x 12 Reps - Seated Leg Press
  • Four Sets x 12 Reps - Barbell Squats
  • Four Sets x 15 Reps - Leg Extensions
  • Three Sets x 10 Reps - Dumbell Walking Lunges
  • Five Sets x 10 Reps - Abdominal Crunches
  • Five Sets x 15 Reps - Leg Raises
